Let's look at more factors that can affect a pet's response to heat or cold.

  • Long-haired breeds are more susceptible to extreme heat
  • Short-haired breeds are more susceptible to extreme cold

Flat-faced (brachycephalic) breeds are also more susceptible to weather effects

  • Bulldog, Pug, Boxer, Shih Tzu dogs
  • Persian, Exotic, Scottish Fold cats

Existing medical conditions can also affect how your pet handles extreme weather.
